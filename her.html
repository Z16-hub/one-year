My Dearest Faith,

One year ago, my world changed in the most beautiful way possible. I didn't just meet someone amazing—I found my person. The one who makes ordinary moments feel like magic, who turns bad days into bearable ones with just a smile.

Thank you for this incredible year. Here's to forever.

Yours completely,
